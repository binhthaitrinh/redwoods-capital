Investment philosophy

We focus on providing our investors the balance of asset preservation with risk-adjusted return and diversification assets that less correlated to market fluctuations. We typical target the following

Stable Performance

Student Housing investment is classified as a global asset class which provides a large, steady population of influx renters anchored by a historically strong-enrollment university. This stability makes student housing practically immune to outside economic forces even during economic downturns.

Legal Liability

Our legal expert team specializes in one thing only; remove and limit all potential legal liabilities and conflicts to our investors such as: title ownership structure, operational member agreement, federal & state law, insurance policy coverage, litigation, etc.

Tax benefits

Our in-house CPA will set up the suitable structure that would benefit the investors to maximize their tax advantages through depreciation and tax deferral techniques.

Passive Investors

Investors can utilize the benefits of an on-site well-executed property management team to earn a stable stream of tax-advantaged passive income quarterly through the Preferred Return distribution ranging from 7-8% annually. You can do the things in life that matter most to you; and that is what makes passive income truly priceless.

Appreciation and Equity Growth

Accelerated appreciation is accomplished through the due diligence of seeking out well-located, low-expense properties in strong rental markets where the tenants help paying down the principal of the loan while investor’s equity in the property grows.
